2023 - 2024

The Project

Wave Energy Converter

The intent with the teams project was to create a wave energy convertor that would be able to mount to existing foundations and develop an established grid. The main target is existing offshore wind farms. These wind farms are an existing area already dedicated to harvesting wind energy, and with the addition of WECs into this system could make the area more energy efficient.

The team is prototyping a WEC with a modular mounting system that will be able to anchor itself on a number of different foundations, developing energy harvesting those areas.
